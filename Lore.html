ZA WARUDO

The League of Legends Universe is split into many different territories, each with a unique identity and history behind it.
The world has a story to relate to almost anyone! Are you a thrill seeking adventurer who loves to sail the seven seas, then Bilgewater is for you.
Are you a knight looking to serve a king and expand your mighty empire, You my friend are a Demacian. Fancy a desert of sand - that's Shurima.
Do you strive for technological prowess or want to rule the under-belly of a city where all sorts of characters appear, then take a visit to Piltover and Zaun.
Perhaps a viking living in the tundra worshipping the old gods, or a half animal protecting nature, maybe your a shadow demon hell bent on destroying the world!
The point is the world is so fast and full of life, there is never a place where you are bored, and wioth over 140 characters in the game each with a unique lore,
There is no such reading up you can do!
